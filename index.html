We need your help!

03/11/25

We are looking for YOUR help with sorting/categorising questions by topic!
If you are able to help, especially with nicher subjects,
please send me a message here or in the contact box with preferably your discord,
or any other way that we can contact.

While for some subjects the resources exist online,
for the subjects I don't study it's unreasonable (or even impossible)
to accurately sort these questions.

All we are looking for is for you to provide a list of topics,
and what questions from past papers belong to them.
It doesn't need to be in some special format, whatever works best for you.
Even if you only do one or two papers, any help is greatly appreciated.
